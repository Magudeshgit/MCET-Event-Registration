Project Presentation

Students shall present their POC or MVP projects to the jury panel. Presentation should include models, working videos and a physical prototype is preferable. (max: 4-5 slides) 6min/team(4min-pitch, 2min-Viva)

Paper Presentation

Student should exhibit their research papers as powerpoint presentations to the jury panel. (max: 4-5slides) 6min per team (4min - pitch, 2min-viva) Note: You will have to tell about your paper in the next generated page.

Product Pitching

Students are expected to enter a product of choice in the next form and they are supposed to show their marketing skills to better pitch the product on a forum of common users. Note: you will have to enter your product of choice in the next page

Ideathon

Ideathon will be conducted in 3 rounds namely "Ideathon Spirit" for problem identification, definition and analysis, Solution Phase for solution proposal and solution development, Final phase where the first two rounds are consolidated and decision to be made by the jury panel.
Theme: Open Innovation in sustainable development goals. problem statements can also be chosen on the spot.
